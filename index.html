---About Us---

Learn about our story and commitment to quality.

Welcome to Arzoo Bakery, a sweet haven where dreams are baked since 1973. Nestled in the heart of confectionery craftsmanship, our journey began with a sprinkle of passion and a dash of nostalgia. Founded by Grandma Arzoo, our secret recipes have been handed down through generations, crafting a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds. From whimsical cupcakes to timeless pies, each creation is a testament to our commitment to delight. Step into Arzoo Bakery and immerse yourself in a world where every bite whispers tales of love, heritage, and the sweet pursuit of happiness. Your joy, our legacy!
